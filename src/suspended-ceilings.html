Suspended Ceilings

We supply & install various types of suspended ceilings using top brands from CCF, SIG and Nevill Long.
15mm and 24mm Exposed grid systems with ceiling tiles to suit specific job requirements. Such as Armstrong Prelude 24 TLS grid with Armstrong Dune Evo 600mm x 600mm tiles
Concealed grid clip in Metal tile ceiling systems. Perforated and non-perforated tiles with Acoustic Fleece. Also Metal lay-in tiles for 24mm exposed grid systems.
Aluminium Grid systems- Axiom C Canopy SL2 by Armstrong and various others.
Plasterboard Casoline MF ceilings and feature bulkheads, Taping and Jointing ready for decoration works. Plasterboards such as fireline / Soundshield or Moisture resistant can be used depending on site requirements. Lighting also supplied and installed into ceilings.
